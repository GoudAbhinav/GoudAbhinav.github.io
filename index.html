About Me

I am a final-year Electrical Engineering student at IIT Gandhinagar with a strong foundation in digital design, embedded systems, and machine learning. My practical experience includes a digital design internship at Texas Instruments, where I developed real-time FPGA-based signal processing systems. I am passionate about hardware acceleration, approximate computing, and creating efficient, real-world solutions.
